About pitch.ideas

pitch.ideas is a student project created as part of a school assignment about founding and developing a startup. This website serves as a minimum viable product (MVP) to demonstrate what such a platform could look like.


All startups, users, pitches, investments, and other content on this website are entirely fictional and exist for demonstration purposes only. No real investment, funding, or financial services are offered through this platform.


Although this project uses a database, it is intended solely for educational purposes. Data may be created, modified, or deleted at any time without notice, and should not be considered permanent or secure.
